🏢 About STRAG™

Built for Nigeria's Transport Reality

Smart Transport Grid Ltd (STRAG™) is a Nigerian technology company providing intelligent and sustainable transport solutions. The company was incorporated in June 2024 under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020.

STRAG operates as a centralised, data-driven transport management system designed to improve safety, accountability, and operational efficiency across public and commercial transport networks in Eastern Nigeria and beyond.

The platform integrates digital identity, cashless payments, compliance tracking, and fleet intelligence into one unified infrastructure layer for state governments, transport operators, and commuters.

🎯

Vision

Making transportation safe, smart, sustainable, and connected for everyone.

🚀

Mission

Empowering cities and communities with safe, intelligent, and sustainable transport solutions.

⚠️ The Challenge

The Transport Sector Challenges

These are not isolated problems. They form an interconnected system of failures that leave passengers unsafe, governments without revenue, and operators without recognition.

📋

Poor Transport Data for Planning

State ministries lack real-time intelligence on fleet size, routes, and ridership patterns, making infrastructure planning unreliable.

🪪

Unverified Operators on the Road

Drivers operate without proper documentation. No certification. No identity trail. No accountability when incidents occur.

⚖️

Weak Traffic Law Enforcement

Violations go unrecorded or are resolved informally through cash. The enforcement chain has no digital audit trail.

📜

Poor Regulatory Systems

Legislative and regulatory frameworks remain outdated. Transport levies and permits are managed manually with no reconciliation.

🚨

Slow Emergency Response

Vehicle breakdowns in remote areas receive no coordinated response. There is no digital alert channel linking operators to emergency services.

🔒

Criminal Activity in Transit

One-chance crimes, kidnapping, robbery, and fake checkpoints flourish because vehicles and passengers have no verified digital identity.

💥

Unsafe Commercial Vehicles

Poorly maintained vehicles operate without inspection records. Accidents result from mechanical failure that proper oversight would have prevented.

🏛️

No Inter-Agency Integration

Police, FRSC, VIO, and state transport ministries operate in silos. Data is not shared. Incidents fall through institutional gaps.

💸

Massive Revenue Leakage

65% of transport revenue is cash-based and untracked.

🔧 The STRAG™ Solutions

One Platform. Every Layer of Transport Covered.

STRAG delivers six integrated solutions that address the full lifecycle of commercial transport — from vehicle registration through to fleet intelligence and revenue collection.

🪪

Commercial Vehicle Registration & Profiling

Every commercial vehicle — buses, keke tricycles, motorcycles — receives a verified digital identity linked to the national NIN system.

  • Unique STRAG ID per vehicle, driver, and owner
  • QR code stickers installed on every vehicle
  • Central database accessible to enforcement agencies
  • Annual renewal with automated reminders
🎓

Commercial Driver and Rider Certification

Annual certification ensures only qualified operators drive passengers across Nigeria, motor parks, and interstate corridors.

  • Digital training and assessment modules
  • Certification card linked to STRAG profile
  • Rating system visible to passengers
  • Automatic suspension for repeated violations
🎟️

Vehicle E-Ticketing System

Electronic invoicing and payment processing for levies, permits, and transport fees. Every naira is tracked with a digital receipt.

  • Digital levy and permit payments
  • Real-time reconciliation with state accounts
  • USSD fallback for feature phone users
  • Auto-generated receipts for operators
🚦

Vehicle Inspection & Enforcement

Enforcement officers digitally book violations linked to the vehicle registry. Disputes are resolved through a transparent, tamper-proof audit trail.

  • Digital offence capture with QR-coded receipts
  • Offenders scan to verify fines and track payment
  • Eliminates cash-based enforcement corruption
💳

Smart Tap & Pay Fare System

Cashless fare collection through NFC terminals and QR code scanning. Designed for urban buses, keke tricycles, and motorcycle operators.

  • NFC tap-and-pay and QR fare collection
  • Digital wallet with daily settlement dashboard
  • Ruggedised hardware for non-smartphone operators
  • Passenger auto-receipt for every journey
🆘

Incident Reporting & Emergency Alerts

GPS-based SOS alerts connect passengers and drivers to emergency services in real time. Live trip sharing deters criminal activity.

  • SOS panic button on passenger and driver apps
  • Live trip sharing with trusted contacts
  • Electronic passenger manifest per journey
  • SOS alerts forwarded to nearest police station

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About STRAG

Answers to the questions most frequently asked by governments, transport operators, investors, and passengers.

What is STRAG and what problem does it solve?

▼
STRAG is a digital public infrastructure platform for Nigeria's informal commercial transport sector. It solves three interconnected failures: passenger insecurity from unverified vehicles, revenue leakage from cash-based operations, and an invisible economy where 60% of commercial vehicles have no digital identity. STRAG registers vehicles, certifies drivers, enables cashless payments, and provides governments with real-time fleet intelligence.

How does STRAG work for drivers who do not have smartphones?

▼
STRAG provides ruggedised dedicated payment terminals for operators without smartphones. The platform also includes a USSD fallback system that enables basic functions — including fare collection and identity verification — through any mobile phone, with or without data connectivity. No driver is excluded due to device limitations.

Which transport types does STRAG cover?

▼
STRAG covers all commercial transport categories relevant to Eastern Nigeria: urban buses operating city routes, keke (tricycle) operators, commercial motorcycle riders (okada), interstate buses running routes such as Enugu to Onitsha, and any vehicle registered to carry passengers or cargo for commercial purposes through motor parks and union terminals.

How does STRAG protect passenger data and privacy?

▼
All personal and transactional data on the STRAG platform is handled in accordance with Nigerian data protection standards. Passenger data is never sold to third parties. Trip data shared with government agencies is aggregated for planning purposes. Emergency alerts are transmitted only with the explicit action of the passenger through the SOS mechanism.

How does STRAG help operators access credit?

▼
By recording every trip and fare digitally, STRAG builds a verifiable income history for each operator. This data forms the basis for a credit scoring profile that STRAG's fintech partners can use to extend microloan products to operators who were previously invisible to the formal financial system. The same trip records also create risk profiles for formal insurance eligibility.

How do enforcement agencies use STRAG?

▼
VIO, FRSC, and Police officers access STRAG through a dedicated enforcement interface. They scan a vehicle QR code to retrieve the full compliance profile instantly. Violations are booked digitally with GPS location and timestamp. Fines are collected directly into state accounts through the payment rails, eliminating cash-based enforcement and the corruption it enables.
